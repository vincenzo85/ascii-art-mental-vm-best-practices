Availability and Distribution

Distribute VMs across multiple zones and regions for high availability.

Choosing the Right Machine Type

  • Compute-Intensive Workloads: CPU-Optimized
  • Memory-Intensive Workloads: Memory-Optimized
  • Graphics-Intensive Workloads: GPU-Enabled

Test different machine types to find the optimal performance.

Cost Management Strategies

  • Committed Use Discounts for constant workloads
  • Preemptible Instances for fault-tolerant and non-critical workloads

Using Labels

Labels help organize and manage your VMs. Include:

  • Environment (development, testing, production)
  • Team responsible for the VM
  • Business unit the VM belongs to

Conclusion

Implement these best practices to optimize your VM performance, availability, and cost-efficiency.
